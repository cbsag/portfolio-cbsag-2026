## Overview
Detect negation and speculation cues in biomedical text with consistent sequence labeling.

## System design
- Models: BERT, BioBERT, ClinicalBERT
- Dataset: BIOSCOPE
- Labeling: BIO tagging with token and span-level checks
- Training/evaluation designed around token and span-level consistency

## Results
- Compared domain-specific vs general-purpose encoder behavior
- Established a strong baseline for later biomedical assertion-quality work
